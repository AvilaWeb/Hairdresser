About us

We understand how important is to look great in order to feel amazing

We were founded in 1965 by Varela, a famous Spanish hairdresser originally from Bilbao. Varela first arrived to Germany with the dream of opening his own hair salon in the city of Dresden. After 5 years working independently, Varela bought our very first location at Radebeul.

From that moment on we have been an internationally recognized hair salon with experts from all around the world fully dedicated on improving our customers image. Bilbao's hair salon is all about improving the outside so you feel better in the inside.
